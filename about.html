  • The Game

  • Dirt Rally 2.0 is a racing video game developed and published by Codemasters. It is the 13th installment in the Colin McRae Rally series and the seventh title to carry the "Dirt" name. As a realistic rally racing game, Dirt Rally 2.0 offers a thrilling and immersive experience for racing enthusiasts and fans of the genre.

  • Vehicles

  • Dirt Rally 2.0 features a wide selection of vehicles, each with its own unique characteristics and handling. The game includes a range of rally cars from different eras, including iconic classics and modern machines. Players can choose from various car classes, such as Group A, Group B, and R5, each offering distinct driving dynamics. The game also introduces the official FIA World Rallycross Championship, featuring supercars specifically designed for intense off-road racing on mixed surfaces.

  • Locations

  • Dirt Rally 2.0 takes players to some of the most challenging and iconic rally locations around the world. The game features multiple rally locations, each with multiple stages and weather conditions that significantly impact gameplay. These locations are meticulously designed to replicate the real-world rally experiences, with attention to detail in terms of both visuals and gameplay. Here are some of the locations included in the game:

    • Argentina

      Set in the vast and diverse landscapes of Argentina, this location offers a mix of gravel and dirt roads. Players will encounter winding trails through dense forests, rocky canyons, and open plains.

    • Australia

      Australia's rally stages are known for their fast, flowing sections and unpredictable surfaces. Players will tackle dusty gravel tracks that wind through stunning coastal vistas, eucalyptus forests, and outback landscapes.

    • New Zealand

      This location captures the picturesque beauty of New Zealand, with its lush greenery, breathtaking mountains, and narrow, twisting roads. Expect challenging gravel tracks and narrow bridges amidst the stunning scenery.

    • Poland

      The Polish stages showcase tight, technical routes with gravel and mud surfaces. Players will navigate through dense forests, farmland, and villages, experiencing narrow tracks and hairpin turns.

    • Spain

      Spain presents a unique challenge with its mix of surfaces, including asphalt and gravel. The stages feature a variety of environments, from narrow mountainous roads to sweeping coastal routes, providing a diverse and demanding racing experience.

    • USA

      The American rally stages feature a blend of terrains, from rocky tracks to sandy trails. Players will race through vast landscapes, including deserts, canyons, and forests, with changing weather conditions adding an extra layer of difficulty.

  • DLC

  • Dirt Rally 2.0 offers a range of downloadable content (DLC) that expands the already thrilling racing experience. These additional content packs introduce new vehicles, locations, and challenges, providing players with fresh opportunities to test their rally skills. Each DLC pack in Dirt Rally 2.0 enhances the game by adding more variety to the vehicle selection and expanding the available rally locations. Whether you're a fan of classic rally cars or prefer the modern beasts, the DLC packs offer something for every racing enthusiast. The additional content injects new life into the game, ensuring that players always have fresh challenges to overcome and new experiences to enjoy. Get ready to immerse yourself in the ultimate rally racing adventure with the exciting DLC packs of Dirt Rally 2.0. Here are some notable DLC packs available for Dirt Rally 2.0:

    • Season 1

      This DLC pack includes a selection of iconic rally cars such as the legendary Citroën C4 Rally and the Volkswagen Polo GTI R5. It also introduces two new rally locations: Monte Carlo and Sweden. Players can now tackle the treacherous snowy tracks of Sweden and the demanding asphalt roads of Monte Carlo.

    • Season 2

      The second DLC pack introduces even more exciting content. It features the historic rally cars Porsche 911 RGT Rally Spec and the Lancia 037 Evo 2, allowing players to experience the raw power of these legendary machines. The locations included in Season 2 are Germany and Finland. Germany presents challenging tarmac stages, while Finland offers fast-paced gravel roads with jumps and crests.

    • Season 3

      Season 3 expands the roster with additional rally vehicles, including the classic Peugeot 206 Rally and the modern-era Skoda Fabia Rally. The DLC also brings two new rally locations: Greece and Wales. Greece offers rugged terrain with gravel and rocky surfaces, while Wales provides forested tracks with unpredictable weather conditions.

    • Season 4

      The final DLC pack for Dirt Rally 2.0 completes the game's content with more thrilling additions. It introduces the powerful Ford Focus RS Rally 2007 and the Subaru Impreza, iconic cars that have left their mark on the rally world. The locations included in Season 4 are Monte Carlo (revisited) and Germany (revisited), offering new variations of these beloved locations.
